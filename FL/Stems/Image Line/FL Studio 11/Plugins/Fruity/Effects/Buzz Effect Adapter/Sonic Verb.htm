Sonic Verb for Buzz

Introduction...

Sonic Verb is a freeware reverb for Buzz.

After hearing numerous complains about Buzz not having a good-enough reverb, Carsten Sørensen and I sat down to port the Sonic Timeworks 4080L to Buzz. So now you have a $399 reverb for Buzz for free :)

If you make any cool stuff with it, we would of course not mind a copy :-)

I'm not sure what version of Buzz you'll need, but I was working on a beta dated 31th of July, 2000, and I'm pretty sure you'll need that as a minimum. This has to do with the reverb being stereo in / stereo out...
